## How We Score: The Value Index

We don't use opinions or star ratings. Every platform gets two scores:

- **Feature coverage** — what's included in the cheapest paid plan (chat, images, voice, video, memory) before any credits or add-ons kick in
- **Price efficiency** — how close the plan is to the cheapest option we've tested

We combine these using a **geometric mean** — the same math the UN uses to rank countries by quality of life. This means a platform can't score high by acing one dimension and failing the other. A great feature set with terrible pricing gets punished, and vice versa.
