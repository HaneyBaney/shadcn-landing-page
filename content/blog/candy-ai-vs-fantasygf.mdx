## The Multimedia Difference

This is where FantasyGF stands out. Voice chat, image generation, and video all work inside the same conversation window. On Candy AI, these are separate features you access individually.

FantasyGF's integrated approach makes conversations feel more dynamic — switching between text, voice, and visuals mid-conversation. Candy AI's features are individually stronger (better image quality, longer video clips) but less seamlessly connected.

## Memory: The Dealbreaker

**Winner: Candy AI (8/10 vs None)**

FantasyGF has **zero memory.** Every session starts fresh. Candy AI remembers your conversations, preferences, and character context across sessions.

This is a fundamental difference. If you use AI companions casually (quick sessions, different characters), the lack of memory may not matter. If you want any sense of ongoing relationship, Candy AI is the only option here.
