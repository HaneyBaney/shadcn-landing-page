## The Catch: Cheap ≠ Best Value

Candy AI is the cheapest at $3.99/mo yearly. But images, voice calls, and video all cost tokens — and you only get 100 free tokens per month. If you generate 20 images (roughly 20 tokens each depending on the model), you're already out.

Compare that to AiAllure at $9.90/mo yearly: unlimited chat, unlimited images, unlimited voice. You pay $5.91 more per month but never worry about running out.

**The Value Index accounts for this.** It scores feature coverage (what's included) × price efficiency (how cheap it is) using a geometric mean. A platform can't rank high just by being cheap if it locks everything behind tokens.

## 1. Candy AI — $3.99/mo Yearly (Cheapest Listed Price)

**Yearly:** $3.99/mo ($47.88/year) · **Monthly:** $13.99/mo

The lowest sticker price in the market. Chat is unlimited, which is the core experience for most users. The token system kicks in for everything else: images, phone calls, and video generation all require tokens.

You get 100 free tokens per month. That covers roughly 5–10 images depending on the model, or a few short phone calls. Heavy media users will burn through tokens quickly.

**The real cost if you use media regularly:** $3.99 base + $10–20 in token purchases = $14–24/month effective cost.

**Choose Candy AI if:** Chat is 90% of your usage and you rarely generate images or video.
