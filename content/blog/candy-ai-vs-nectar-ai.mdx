## Chat & Roleplay

**General chat: Tied (8/10)**
Both platforms handle standard conversation competently. Character consistency is good on both. Neither matches the top tier (OurDream 9/10, GPTGirlfriend 9.5/10).

**Structured roleplay: Nectar AI wins**
Nectar's scenario builder is the differentiator. You define settings, character rules, narrative structure, and pacing — then the AI follows those constraints. Within structured scenarios, Nectar's chat quality jumps to 9/10 because the scenario focuses the AI's responses.

Candy AI has no equivalent tool. Roleplay works but is open-ended and relies on you to drive the narrative through prompts.

If roleplay is secondary to you, this comparison is a wash. If roleplay is why you use AI companions, Nectar's scenario builder is a genuine advantage worth the extra cost.

## Image Generation

**Tied on quality (8/10), but Nectar has anime**

Both platforms produce good images at similar quality levels. The key difference: Nectar offers **anime and 2.5D art styles** alongside realistic output. Candy AI is realistic-only.

If you prefer anime aesthetics, Nectar is one of the very few platforms that does this well. If you only want realistic images, either platform works.

Both use credit/token systems for image generation, so heavy users pay more on both.

## Video Generation

**Roughly tied (~20 seconds, 8/10)**
