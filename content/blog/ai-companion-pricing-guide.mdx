## Hidden Costs to Watch For

- **Token/coin systems** — Candy AI, GPTGirlfriend, and MyDreamCompanion use tokens or coins for media features. Heavy users may spend 2–3x the base subscription on extra tokens.
- **Long video generation** — AiAllure charges AllureCoins for videos over 8 seconds. OurDream AI includes 10 clips but charges dreamcoins beyond that.
- **Auto-renewal** — Every platform auto-renews yearly plans. Set a calendar reminder 30 days before renewal to evaluate.
- **Premium tiers** — GPTGirlfriend locks voice behind a Premium tier. MyDreamCompanion's Ultimate plan ($24.99/yr) is needed for full access.
