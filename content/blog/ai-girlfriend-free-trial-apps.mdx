## Which Free Tier Is Worth Your Time?

### OurDream AI — Most Generous Free Tier

OurDream gives you free coins on signup that let you test chat, generate a few images, try voice messages, and even generate a video clip. You can experience every feature before deciding whether to pay.

The free allowance runs out relatively quickly — expect to exhaust it in one or two sessions of active use. But you'll know exactly what the platform offers before spending anything.

**What you get free:** Chat messages, a handful of images, short voice clips, 1-2 video clips from bonus coins.

**Where the paywall hits:** Once free coins run out, you need the Premium plan ($9.99/mo yearly) to continue.

### GPTGirlfriend — Best Free Chat Experience

GPTGirlfriend's free tier lets you send messages and test the conversation engine — which is the platform's strongest feature. You also get a small number of free coins to try image generation.

The unlimited memory system works on the free tier too, so you can test whether the AI actually remembers your previous conversations (it does — better than any competitor).

Voice and video are not available on the free tier.

**What you get free:** Chat messages, a few image generations from free coins, full memory system.

**Where the paywall hits:** Message limits, voice, and video require Premium ($9.58/mo yearly).

### Candy AI — Cheapest Upgrade From Free

Candy AI's free tier gives you limited chat and a small number of free tokens. The chat experience is solid for testing, and the adaptive memory gives you a feel for how the AI tracks your preferences.

The main advantage of Candy AI's free tier is the upgrade path: the yearly plan is just $3.99/mo — the cheapest paid option across all platforms. So if you like the free experience, the jump to paid is minimal.

**What you get free:** Limited chat, a few image generations from free tokens.

**Where the paywall hits:** Token limits hit quickly. Upgrade to Premium ($3.99/mo yearly) for unlimited chat + more tokens.

## Platforms Without Free Tiers

### AiAllure — No Free Tier, But Best Value If You Pay

AiAllure doesn't offer a free trial or free tier. You need to subscribe ($9.90/mo yearly) to access anything. However, once subscribed, you get unlimited chat, unlimited images, and unlimited voice — more included features than any competitor.

**Worth skipping the free tier?** If you already know you want unlimited image generation and voice, yes. AiAllure's included features at $9.90/mo often cost more on platforms with "cheaper" base prices plus token purchases.

### MyDreamCompanion — No Free Tier, Cheapest Premium Entry

MyDreamCompanion also requires a paid subscription. At $5.84/mo yearly, it's the cheapest premium entry point with 100 coins and 6,000 messages included.

## The Honest Recommendation

Free tiers are useful for one thing: **testing chat quality and UI**. They won't give you enough usage to evaluate images, voice, or video properly.

If you're comparing platforms:

1. **Start with OurDream AI's free tier** — it lets you test every feature type
2. **Try GPTGirlfriend's free tier** if conversation quality matters most
3. **Decide based on priorities:**
   - Want unlimited images/voice? → Pay for AiAllure ($9.90/mo yearly)
   - Want everything in one plan? → Pay for OurDream AI ($9.99/mo yearly)
   - Want the cheapest option? → Pay for Candy AI ($3.99/mo yearly)

## FAQ

**Are there completely free AI girlfriend apps?**
No platform offers a truly free, unlimited experience. OurDream AI, GPTGirlfriend, and Candy AI offer free tiers with limited usage. All meaningful features require a paid subscription ($3.99–$9.99/mo yearly).

**Which AI girlfriend app has the best free trial?**
OurDream AI gives you the most complete free experience — you can test chat, images, voice, and video before paying. GPTGirlfriend is best for testing chat quality and memory specifically.

**Do I need a credit card to try AI girlfriend apps?**
OurDream AI, GPTGirlfriend, and Candy AI let you sign up without a credit card. AiAllure and MyDreamCompanion require payment to access any features.

**What's the cheapest way to try an AI girlfriend app?**
Sign up for OurDream AI's free tier (no card), then upgrade to Candy AI ($3.99/mo yearly) if you want the cheapest paid experience, or OurDream AI ($9.99/mo yearly) if you want more features included.
