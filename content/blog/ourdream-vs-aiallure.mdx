**Winner: OurDream AI (9/10 vs 7.5/10)**

AiAllure's chat is functional but clearly secondary to its visual features. Conversations are adequate — the AI stays in character and responds appropriately — but lack the depth, nuance, and emotional range that OurDream (or GPTGirlfriend) delivers.

**Specific differences we observed:**
- OurDream handles topic transitions organically; AiAllure's transitions feel more mechanical
- OurDream picks up on emotional cues and subtext; AiAllure responds more literally
- OurDream maintains narrative threads across long conversations; AiAllure loses depth after 15-20 minutes
- OurDream's roleplay feels collaborative; AiAllure's feels reactive

If you primarily use AI companions for conversation, OurDream (or GPTGirlfriend at $15/mo) is a much better choice. AiAllure's chat works as a complement to its video features but is not a selling point on its own.
