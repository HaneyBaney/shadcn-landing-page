We tested 5 platforms with paid accounts and documented exactly what video you get — and what costs extra.

## The Key Question: Included vs. Pay-Per-Clip

Only two platforms include any video in the base subscription:

- **AiAllure** includes short clips (up to 8 seconds, ~3 per session) at no extra cost. Longer videos (10–120 seconds) require AllureCoins.
- **OurDream AI** includes 10 video clips per month from the shared dreamcoin pool (1,000 coins/mo).

The other three platforms — Candy AI, GPTGirlfriend, and MyDreamCompanion — charge tokens or coins for every video clip. If video is a priority, this distinction matters more than the sticker price.

## 1. AiAllure — Best Video Overall

**$9.90/mo yearly** · Short clips free, long clips cost AllureCoins

AiAllure is the only platform capable of generating videos up to 120 seconds. Short clips (up to 8 seconds) are included in the subscription at no extra cost. For longer videos (10–120 seconds), you need AllureCoins.

Quality is excellent at 20–30 seconds — consistent face identity, realistic lighting, natural physics. At 60+ seconds, expect minor artifacts. At 120 seconds, quality drops but remains impressive for the technology.

The recently launched **Live Rooms** feature adds real-time video interaction, which no competitor offers yet.

**Video verdict:** Best duration, best quality, and short clips are free. The only real option if you want videos longer than 10 seconds.

## 2. OurDream AI — Most Predictable Video Allowance

**$9.99/mo yearly** · 10 videos/mo from shared coin pool

OurDream includes 10 video clips per month as part of the 1,000 dreamcoin monthly allowance. You know exactly what you get — no surprise charges. The clips are shorter than AiAllure's maximum but the quality is solid.

Chat, images (200/mo), voice (20 min/mo), and video are all bundled in one plan. If you want a little bit of everything without managing separate token purchases, this is the simplest option.

**Video verdict:** 10 clips/mo included, predictable allowance, good quality for shorter clips.

## 3. Candy AI — Cheapest Entry, Video Costs Tokens

**$3.99/mo yearly** · Video generation costs tokens

Candy AI is the cheapest platform at $3.99/mo yearly, but video generation requires tokens. You get 100 free tokens per month — how many videos that buys depends on the clip length and model.

If you mostly want chat with occasional video as a bonus, the token cost is manageable. If video is a primary use case, you'll need to buy token packs regularly.

**Video verdict:** Available but not included. Budget-friendly for light video usage.
