## How We Tested AiAllure

We subscribed to AiAllure with our own money on the yearly plan ($16.40/month). We tested it for 30 days using the same standardized prompts, scenarios, and evaluation criteria we apply to every platform. During the same period, we ran identical tests on OurDream AI, Candy AI, GPTGirlfriend, and MyDreamCompanion for direct comparison.

We focused heavily on video generation — duration limits, quality at different lengths, face consistency, physics, rendering speed — since this is AiAllure's primary differentiator. We also evaluated chat quality, image generation, memory, and overall value.

## Getting Started with AiAllure

AiAllure offers a free tier that lets you explore the platform and test basic features before committing to a subscription. The signup process is straightforward — email registration, character selection or creation, and you are in.

The interface is polished and visually oriented. It is clear from the first screen that this platform prioritizes visual output — image and video generation are front and center in the UI, with chat as a supporting feature rather than the primary focus.

Character customization is solid. You can adjust appearance, style, and personality. The platform offers both pre-made characters and custom creation. We found the pre-made options to be high quality and diverse, which is useful if you want to start generating content quickly without extensive setup.

## Image Generation: 9/10

AiAllure's image generation is excellent — among the best we have tested. Output quality is consistently high with realistic lighting, detailed textures, and strong character consistency across multiple generations.

The platform clearly invests heavily in its visual generation pipeline. Images feel polished and professional compared to the occasionally inconsistent output from Candy AI or the more basic results from GPTGirlfriend.

Customization options are robust. You can control pose, setting, style, clothing, and expression with good precision. The AI interprets prompts accurately and rarely produces off-target results.

## Chat Quality: 7.5/10

AiAllure's chat is functional and adequate, but it is not the platform's strength. Conversations are competent — the AI maintains character and responds appropriately to most prompts — but there is a noticeable quality gap compared to GPTGirlfriend or OurDream AI.

**What works:**
- Character consistency is maintained during chat
- The AI handles casual conversation and basic roleplay well
- Response speed is fast
- Chat integrates with the visual generation features smoothly

**Where it falls short:**
- Conversation depth is limited compared to GPTGirlfriend (9.5/10) or OurDream (9/10)
- Extended roleplay sessions can feel repetitive
- Emotional range is narrower than top chat-focused platforms
- The AI occasionally defaults to generic responses for nuanced prompts

If you are choosing a platform primarily for conversation, GPTGirlfriend or OurDream AI are both significantly better at a lower price. AiAllure's chat is best understood as a complement to its video and image capabilities, not as a standalone selling point.

## Memory: 7/10

AiAllure retains basic conversation context and character preferences across sessions. It works — the AI remembers who your character is and general conversation themes — but it is not as detailed or reliable as OurDream AI or MyDreamCompanion.

During our testing, the AI sometimes lost specific details from earlier sessions, particularly after gaps of several days. For users who prioritize ongoing relationship continuity, this is a notable weakness. For users who primarily generate visual content with chat as a secondary feature, the memory system is adequate.

## Voice Features: 7/10

Voice messages are available with decent quality. The AI maintains character personality in voice output. However, voice is clearly a secondary feature — quality and naturalness are below what OurDream AI offers, and there are no real-time voice calls.
