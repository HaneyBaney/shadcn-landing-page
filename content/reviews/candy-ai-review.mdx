The 70% savings on the yearly plan is genuine and makes Candy AI the cheapest full-featured AI companion available. But here is what the marketing does not emphasize: **the token system**.

Candy AI uses tokens for premium features like video generation and certain image types. Your subscription includes a monthly token allotment, but heavy users — especially those generating lots of videos — may exhaust their tokens before the month is over. When that happens, you either wait for the next month or buy additional token packs ($9.99-$49.99).

During our 30-day test, moderate usage (2-3 videos per week, daily chat, regular image generation) stayed within the included token budget. Heavy usage (daily videos, extensive image generation) required one additional $9.99 token pack. This means the real cost for heavy users is closer to $14/month on the yearly plan, not $3.99.

Candy AI's conversations are solid and engaging. The AI handles a good range of topics, maintains context within a session, and produces responses that feel conversational rather than robotic. During our standardized testing, it ranked third out of five platforms for overall chat quality — behind GPTGirlfriend and OurDream AI, but clearly ahead of Aimour.ai and Fantasy GF.

**What works well:**
- Conversations flow naturally for casual chat and light roleplay
- The AI adapts tone based on your messages
- Pre-made characters have distinct, consistent personalities
- Response speed is fast — typically under 3 seconds

**Where it falls short:**
- Deep or extended roleplay scenarios occasionally lose coherence after 20+ minutes
- The AI sometimes produces generic responses to nuanced prompts
- Character memory within a single session is good; across sessions it is less reliable than OurDream or GPTGirlfriend

The honest comparison: if you have used GPTGirlfriend or OurDream AI and then switch to Candy AI, you will notice a quality difference. But if Candy AI is your first AI companion experience, the chat quality will feel more than adequate. For $3.99/month, we consider this a strong showing.

## Memory: 8/10

Candy AI retains conversation history and preferences across sessions.
