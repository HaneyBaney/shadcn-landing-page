## How We Tested It

Same process as always: our own subscription, standardized prompts, 30-day test alongside 8 other platforms. We tested the free tier, the Pro plan ($6.67/mo yearly), and explored the guided scenario system extensively.

## Getting Started

Secret Desires has a free tier — basic chat access to evaluate the platform before committing. Signing up is quick (email or social login) and the interface immediately suggests scenarios to try.

This is where Secret Desires differentiates: **the onboarding is designed for people who have never used AI companions.** Instead of staring at a blank chat wondering what to type, you get:

- Pre-built scenario templates across genres
- Guided prompts that help you navigate conversations
- Progressive difficulty — start simple, unlock more complex scenarios
- Character suggestions based on your stated interests

For experienced users, this hand-holding may feel unnecessary. But for newcomers, it eliminates the "blank page paralysis" that kills engagement on other platforms.

---

## The Guided Scenario System

Secret Desires structures conversations around pre-built scenarios with clear narratives, character roles, and progression paths. Think of it as curated roleplay experiences vs the open-ended chat of other platforms.

**What works well:**
- Scenarios are well-written with clear narrative arcs
- The AI follows scenario rules consistently
- Progressive unlocking keeps the experience fresh over time
- Genre variety — romance, fantasy, mystery, drama, slice-of-life

**How it compares to competitors:**
- Nectar AI ($10/mo) — Nectar's scenario builder lets you *design* custom scenarios; Secret Desires gives you *pre-built* ones. Nectar for creators, Secret Desires for consumers.
- SpicyChat ($12.99/mo) — SpicyChat has community characters; Secret Desires has guided scenarios. Both offer variety, but through different mechanisms.
- GPTGirlfriend ($15/mo) — GPTGirlfriend's roleplay is deeper and more flexible but unstructured. Secret Desires' guided approach is easier for beginners.

## Chat Quality: 7.5/10

General conversation quality is adequate but not a selling point. The AI handles chat competently within structured scenarios — staying in character, following rules, producing appropriate responses. Outside of guided scenarios, free-form chat feels less polished than the top competitors.

**Within guided scenarios:** The AI shines because the scenario structure constrains and focuses the responses. Chat feels purposeful and engaging.

**Outside guided scenarios:** Conversations are functional but lack the natural flow and emotional range of OurDream (9/10) or GPTGirlfriend (9.5/10). The AI feels more reactive than proactive.

For users who primarily interact through the guided scenario system, the 7.5/10 chat score is somewhat misleading — the structured experience feels closer to 8/10 in practice. The score reflects the overall chat engine quality across both guided and unstructured use.

## Voice Messages: 7/10

Secret Desires offers voice messages — not real-time voice calls, but recorded voice interactions. Quality is decent, and the AI maintains character personality in voice output. Voice adds another layer to the guided scenarios.

No real-time voice calls (only OurDream and FantasyGF offer that among our reviewed platforms).

---

## Memory: 8/10

Secret Desires retains conversation history and character preferences across sessions. The AI remembers scenario progress, established character dynamics, and user preferences. Memory works well enough that returning to a scenario after days feels continuous rather than disconnected.

This is a genuine advantage over FantasyGF (no memory) and AiAllure (7/10). It trails OurDream and MyDreamCompanion (both 9/10) but is solid for the price point.

## Pricing: Three Affordable Tiers

| Plan | Monthly | Annual (per month) | What You Get |
|------|---------|-------------------|-------------|
| Free | $0 | — | Basic chat, limited scenarios |
| Pro | $7.99/mo | $6.67/mo | Full scenarios, image gen, hearts system |
| Ultra | $13.99/mo | $11.67/mo | More hearts, higher quality |
| Max | $19.99/mo | $16.67/mo | Most hearts, best quality |

The **Pro plan at $6.67/month yearly** is the sweet spot. You get full access to guided scenarios, image generation, video generation, voice messages, and memory. The Ultra and Max tiers add more "hearts" (the platform's credit system) for heavier usage.

**Value assessment:** At $6.67/month, Secret Desires is one of the most affordable full-feature platforms:
- Cheaper than OurDream ($12.99/mo), GPTGirlfriend ($15/mo), Nectar AI ($10/mo), SpicyChat ($12.99/mo)
- Comparable to Candy AI ($3.99/mo yearly) and MyDreamCompanion ($5.84/mo yearly)
- More expensive than nothing — no free tier match for Candy AI's free trial

The hearts system means very heavy users may need Ultra or Max, but moderate use stays within Pro limits.

## How It Compares

**vs Candy AI ($3.99/mo yearly):** Candy AI is cheaper, has better images, and a no-CC free trial. Secret Desires has guided scenarios and slightly better memory. Choose Candy AI for value; choose Secret Desires if you want structured guidance.

**vs OurDream ($12.99/mo):** OurDream is better across every feature category but costs twice as much. If budget matters, Secret Desires delivers a solid experience at half the price.

**vs Nectar AI ($10/mo):** Nectar has the scenario *builder* for creating custom roleplay. Secret Desires has pre-built guided scenarios. Nectar for experienced roleplay creators; Secret Desires for guided consumers.

**vs SpicyChat ($12.99/mo):** SpicyChat has more characters and better chat. Secret Desires has images, video, voice, and memory — features SpicyChat completely lacks. Secret Desires is the better-rounded platform.

## Who Is This For?

**Choose Secret Desires if you:**
- Are new to AI companions and want a guided experience
- Want a full-feature platform at a budget price
- Prefer structured scenarios over open-ended chat
- Want images, video, AND voice at under $7/month

## Final Verdict: 4.5/5

Secret Desires found its lane: affordable, full-featured, and beginner-friendly. The guided scenario system solves a real problem — most AI companion platforms assume you know what you are doing. Secret Desires does not.

At $6.67/month yearly with images, video, voice, and memory included, the value is genuinely strong. Experienced users will outgrow the guided approach, but newcomers will appreciate the structured onboarding that no other platform offers.
